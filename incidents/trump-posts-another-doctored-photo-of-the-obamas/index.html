Trump posts another doctored photo of the Obamas

US President Donald Trump posted a falsified image of Barack and Michelle Obama waving before boarding an Air Force One that had been digitally altered to include graffiti. The manipulated image depicted the Obamas smiling on the stairs of the presidential plane, which featured spray-painted text including the campaign slogan 'Yes We Can', the name 'Obama', 'BLM', and Arabic text. This incident occurred after Trump previously posted a racist image of the Obamas as primates, which was deleted following bipartisan backlash. The article notes that the use of graffiti in this context is a coded message often used in racist messaging against Black people.
