Sex offender handed suspended jail sentence for possessing AI-generated image

A 63-year-old registered sex offender, Andrew Jenkins, was sentenced at Merthyr Tydfil Crown Court for possessing an AI-generated image of a naked girl aged between 12 and 14 on his mobile phone and breaching a Sexual Harm Prevention Order (SHPO). During a routine police visit in October 2024, officers discovered the prohibited image among 16 other prohibited images of children found on a device Jenkins had not previously disclosed. Jenkins admitted possessing the image and breaching the court order, with a judge noting his inability to manage deviant thoughts. He received a suspended prison sentence of 10 months for the SHPO breach and two months for the prohibited image possession, along with a new five-year SHPO and a requirement to complete rehabilitation activities.
