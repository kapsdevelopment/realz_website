Fake photo of a cobra triggers major emergency response in Germany

A reported sighting of a cobra in a ditch at a tree nursery in Halstenbek, northern Germany, triggered a major fire service operation involving 40 firefighters and drones. A snake expert could not rule out the possibility of a real cobra based on a photograph provided, leading to a large-scale search and public warning. It later emerged that the photograph was AI-generated by a son who wanted to frighten his father, both employees at the nursery. The deputy fire chief expressed relief that it was a fake but noted the significant resources wasted on the false alarm.
