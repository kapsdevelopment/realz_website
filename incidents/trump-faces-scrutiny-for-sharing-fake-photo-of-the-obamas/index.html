Trump Faces Scrutiny For Sharing Fake Photo Of The Obamas

On July 5, 2026, Donald Trump shared a digitally altered image on Truth Social depicting former President Barack Obama and former First Lady Michelle Obama waving before boarding an Air Force One covered in graffiti. The image included fake graffiti with slogans such as 'Yes We Can,' 'Obama,' 'BLM,' and the Arabic phrase 'alhamdulillah.' This post followed a previous incident in February 2026 where Trump shared an altered image portraying the Obamas as primates, which was removed after bipartisan criticism. Trump has also shared other manipulated images, including one from June showing Obama's presidential library surrounded by garbage.
