Use cases

Where verified photos can reduce doubt

Realz is a verified camera app for moments where a photo is meant to document something real. Instead of asking people to trust a screenshot, caption, or edited image file, Realz helps create a cryptographic proof at capture time.

Capture-first proof

Realz does not try to detect whether any old image is fake. It helps users capture a new photo in Realz, create a proof, and share a verification link others can inspect.

Journalism & field reporting

Use Realz when published images need a stronger signal that the photo started as an in-app capture.

Financial security

Use verified photos for expenses, insurance moments, goods, deliveries, and other high-trust image claims.

Dating & social identity

Ask for a Realz photo when identity, recency, or heavy filtering creates uncertainty.

Events & experiences

Preserve real moments with a verifiable record that can be checked later.

Professional documentation

Support review, audit, and documentation workflows where photo integrity matters.

Social sharing with trust

Share real moments online with a public proof instead of asking people to guess what is edited or synthetic.

C2PA-compatible exports

Content Credentials for verified photos

Realz can add embedded, C2PA-compatible Content Credentials to verified exports so proof links and integrity metadata can travel with image files in compatible tools and workflows.
