AI-Generated Image and Video Falsely Shared as Real Footage of Ayatollah Khamenei's Funeral - Fact Crescendo Sri Lanka English | The leading fact-checking website

In early July 2026, images and videos circulated online claiming to show the funeral of Iran's Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei. Fact-checking by srilanka.factcrescendo.com determined that these visuals were generated using artificial intelligence rather than depicting genuine scenes from the funeral. The viral image displayed distorted non-Latin text and an invisible SynthID watermark, while the video showed architectural inconsistencies with Tehran's Grand Mosalla Mosque. Independent analysis using OpenAI's verification tool and Hive Moderation's AI detection systems confirmed the materials were AI-generated. While Khamenei's funeral ceremonies did take place in early July 2026, the specific visuals circulating online were not authentic documentation of the events.
